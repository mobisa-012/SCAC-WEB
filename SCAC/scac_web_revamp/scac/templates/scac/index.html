About Us

We are driven by the passion for excellence through teamwork in service delivery predicated on the following values:

Goals

  • To advise on the means of creating an enabling environment for resource mobilization by commercial state corporations to minimize their dependence on exchequer funding
  • To ensure that State Corporations adopt best management practices
  • To ensure improved performance of State Corporations based on initiation and implementation of performance based contracts
  • To reinforce transparency and accountability in the management of State Corporations
  • To ensure the development of appropriate terms and conditions of service for staff and officials of State Corporations
  • To act as a catalyst in the process of reform and reorganization of the State Corporations sector.
